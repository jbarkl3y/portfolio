Welcome

To the portfolio of Jason Barkley

Introduction

I am an intrepid traveler who loves fly fishing, the outdoors, and technology. I work as a System Administrator as well as a freelance web developer.

I also love bluegrass music and play the Guitar, Banjo, and Mandolin. I play in a family band that my brother and I started called The Fellow Travelers.

Travel and exploring the outdoors is one of my great passions in life. When I am out deep in a forest or high on a mountain top I get some of the best inspiration to help me achieve my professional and personal goals.

Where I'm From

I come from a logging town called Longview, WA. It resides in the Southwestern part of the state about 30 minutes inland from the coast. I love being in the forests or exploring old logging roads. Growing up in a mill town you learn hard work and dedication early on in your life.

I graduated from BYU-Idaho with a B.S. in Computer Information Technology.
